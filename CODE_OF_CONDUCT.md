# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free community for everyone participating in Arco, regardless of background, identity, experience level, or personal characteristics.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in technical disagreement.
- Assume good intent while still being precise about correctness and risk.
- Give and receive feedback professionally.
- Focus on repository health, maintainability, and user impact.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form.
- Personal attacks, insults, or sustained disruptive behavior.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Deliberate sabotage of collaboration channels or project infrastructure.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all Arco project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, documentation, and synchronous community channels.

## Reporting

Report incidents to `conduct@daxis.io`.

Please include:

- A description of what happened.
- Links/screenshots/logs if available.
- Relevant timestamps and participants.

All reports are reviewed promptly and handled with discretion.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for fair and consistent enforcement. Possible responses include:

- Private warning.
- Temporary moderation actions.
- Temporary or permanent removal from community spaces.
- Contribution restrictions for severe or repeated violations.
